Who we are

We are a team of innovative developers, whose care for female and upcoming mothers has led to to creative solutions that solve the deleterous problem of gestational related Amnesia(memory loss)

We also stand to support you throughout your Trimesters and even beyond

Our mission

our mission is to help pregnant ladies and women to be the best version of themselves through healthy living and lifestyles

Our vision

To reduce the effect of gestational related Amnesia through constant reminders of routines, schedules, chores and so on throughout the course of pregnancy

our core values

  • Optimism
  • healthy living and lifestyle
  • Empathy
